About the Event

Punjab College of Technical Education, Ludhiana in association with Innovation Mission Punjab organized an Innovation Challenge with the aim to find the next big entrepreneur from Punjab. The event reached out to students and working professionals and created a wave of innovation that echoed throughout the state as it saw around 200 teams participating from different schools and colleges out of which 22 were shortlisted. The Innovation Challenge was open to all and saw participants from various schools, colleges & Universities neighboring Ludhiana in Punjab and presented their idea through their elevator pitch video of their idea and business model.

the jury members were Mr. Sahil Makkar, Founder of Punjab Angels Network, Mr. Abhay Singhal, founder of TickTalkTo and an alumnus of IIT Bombay Ms Aanchal Sood, Head Pollinator at Innovation Mission Punjab and Mr. Naresh Aggarwal, Dean Corporate RelationsPunjab College of Technical Education
