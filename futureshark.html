What is Future Shark?

Future Business Shark The event “Future Business Sharks ” was envisioned as an energetic and competitive platform to promote innovation and entrepreneurial thinking among students. The session commenced with an inspiring welcome address highlighting the need for student-driven startups in India’s growing economy. Participants got a chance to pitch innovative business ideas in front of a panel of mentors, startup founders, and corporate guests.
