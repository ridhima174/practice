Startup Bootcamp

A 3-day journey of innovation, mentorship, and pitching brilliance at PCTE.

About the Bootcamp

The Startup Bootcamp is a 3-day entrepreneurial immersion where students transform their ideas into potential ventures. Participants receive mentorship from industry leaders, attend interactive workshops, and collaborate to validate and pitch their startups.

The Bootcamp fosters creativity, innovation, and teamwork — empowering students to become the next generation of entrepreneurs.

Program Structure

DAY – 1

First Half

Sessions

  • Understanding Start-up Journey
  • Ideation – Key to Start-up Success

Second Half

Workshop

Start-up Teams will do the ideation for their idea and create a proper Business Plan.

Task

Based on the ideation they will validate with their family / friends / prospects.

DAY – 2

First Half

Sessions

  • How to set up your Start-ups?
  • How to scale up your Start-ups?

Second Half

Workshop

Start-up Teams will build their prototype / MVP and create complete Business Models.

Session

Briefing about “How to create a powerful pitch?”

Task

Create their Pitch Decks.

DAY – 3

First Half

Workshop

Start-up Teams will finalise their Pitch Decks.

Pitching Session

Present to the facilitators and the faculty members.

Second Half

Pitching Session

Shortlisted start-ups will pitch to the jury and investor panel.

Task

Prepare for your LAUNCH.

Why Bootcamp?

The Startup Bootcamp at PCTE (28–30 Nov 2022) gave participants real startup experience—turning ideas into MVPs, refining pitches, and connecting with mentors and investors. It empowered students to transform concepts into actionable business plans and gain entrepreneurial confidence.

Through hands-on mentorship, structured modules, and community-driven growth, the Bootcamp ensures that every participant walks away with the confidence, skills, and network to turn their vision into a viable venture.
